About the Hernandez Greehouse

The Hernandez Greehouse is a small business idea created by Maria, who has a great love for gardening. She time everyday looking after her plants by making sure they get the adequate sunlight and water needed to grow. This hobbie has led her to grow many different kinds of plants including but not limited to basil, mint, apazote, tomatoes, bananas, and the list goes on. The goal with this idea is to provide afordable prices for plants. Maria wants to provide an economical alternative to paying for your plants.
