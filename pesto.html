Pesto

I created this webpage to share the recipes that I use often and that I love.

click here for a more in depth recipe

You need the following for your pesto recipe:

  • 2 cups of fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2/3 cups of extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt
  • Freshly grated parmesan is optional

Steps for making your pesto:

  1. You want to get your garlic grated by placing your cloves or garlic into your food processor.
  2. Next you want to add half of your oil into the food processor along with half of your basil leaves.
  3. Add what is left of the oil and basil. Lastly to finish the pesto add salt and the cheese to taste.
